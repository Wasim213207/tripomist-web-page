Spiti Valley Expedition

Journey through the high-altitude desert of the Himalayas. Experience ancient monasteries, surreal landscapes, and the raw beauty of the middle land.

calendar_clock Duration 6 Days, 5 Nights
groups Group Size 12-15 Explorers
terrain Difficulty Moderate
star Rating 4.9 (120+ reviews)

Arrive in the beautiful hill station of Manali. Spend the day acclimatizing to the altitude. We'll have a brief orientation session in the evening, followed by a welcome dinner with the group. Explore the local cafes in Old Manali if you feel up to it.

An early start as we cross the engineering marvel, Atal Tunnel. Witness the dramatic change in landscape as we enter the Lahaul Valley and proceed towards Kaza, the sub-divisional headquarters of Spiti. The drive offers stunning views of rugged mountains and the Chandra River.

Visit the iconic Key Monastery, perched on a hilltop like a fortress. Later, drive to Kibber, one of the highest inhabited villages in the world. Keep an eye out for Himalayan wildlife, including the elusive Snow Leopard during winter months.
